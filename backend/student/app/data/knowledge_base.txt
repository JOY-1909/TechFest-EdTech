YUVA SETU KNOWLEDGE BASE & GUIDELINES

SECTION 1: PLATFORM GUIDELINES
1. Eligibility
- Students currently enrolled in a recognized university or college in India are eligible.
- You must hold a valid student ID card.
- The minimum age requirement is 18 years.

2. Application Process
- Go to the "Internships" tab in the navigation bar.
- Browse the available opportunities. You can filter by sector (Technology, Public Policy, etc.).
- Click the "Apply Now" button on an internship card.
- Submit your profile details.
- Constraint: You can apply to a maximum of 3 internships simultaneously.

3. Duration & Stipend
- Standard internship duration is between 8 weeks to 6 months.
- Stipends are decided by the individual employer and are paid monthly.
- Unpaid internships are permitted but must offer a certificate of completion.
- Stipend amounts are listed on the internship details page.

4. Code of Conduct
- Maintain professionalism, punctuality, and integrity.
- Respect employer's confidentiality and intellectual property.
- Misconduct may lead to immediate termination and blacklisting from the Yuva Setu platform.

SECTION 2: HOW-TO GUIDES & NAVIGATION

Q: How do I change the language of the portal?
A: 
1. Look at the top right corner of the Navbar.
2. Click on the dropdown menu that shows the current language name (e.g., "English" or "Hindi").
3. Select your preferred language (English or Hindi) from the list.
4. The website content will update immediately.

Q: How do I raise a support request or complaint?
A:
1. Go to the Navbar (top of the page).
2. Hover over the "Support" menu item.
3. Click on "Raise Request" in the dropdown.
4. A modal form will appear. Enter the Subject of your issue and a detailed Message.
5. Click "Submit". You will receive a Ticket ID.

Q: How do I track my existing support request?
A:
1. Go to the Navbar.
2. Hover over "Support".
3. Click on "Track Request".
4. You will be taken to a page listing your submitted tickets and their status (Open/Resolved).

Q: How do I edit my profile?
A:
1. Click on your Avatar/Profile icon in the top right corner.
2. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown.
3. You will be redirected to the multi-step form to update your details.

Q: Where can I find the official Platform Guidelines PDF?
A:
You can download it by clicking "Guidelines" in the Navbar menu or from the "Resources" section in the Footer.

Q: What happens if I forget my password?
A:
Currently, you should contact support@yuvasetu.com or use the "Raise Request" feature if you are logged in. (Password reset feature coming soon).

SECTION 3: CERTIFICATION & BENEFITS
- Upon successful completion of an internship, you are eligible for a digital certificate.
- NOTE: The specific certification details are handled by the employer, but Yuva Setu verifies the completion.
